Dr. Pothireddy Surendranath Reddy

MBBS, MS (Orthopaedics), Fellowship in Joint Replacement

Orthopaedic Surgeon | Arthroscopy & Joint Replacement Specialist

Dr. P. Surendranath Reddy is a highly skilled Orthopaedic Surgeon with over 14 years of clinical, surgical, and academic experience, specializing in Joint Replacement, Arthroscopy, and Trauma Surgery. He is known for patient-centric care, evidence-based practice, and commitment to medical education.

He completed MBBS from SV Medical College, Tirupati (2006), MS Orthopaedics from Kurnool Medical College (2011), and Fellowship in Joint Replacement from Srikara Hospital, Secunderabad (2015). He currently serves as Associate Professor of Orthopaedics at PMRIMS.
